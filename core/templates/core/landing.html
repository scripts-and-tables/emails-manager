Features

Everything multi-mailbox should be.

Designed for people juggling personal, work, and side-project addresses across providers. No clutter, no compromises on privacy.

Unified inbox

Read mail from every connected account in one chronological feed. Filter by 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days.

Any IMAP provider

mail.ru, Gmail, Yandex, Outlook, ProtonMail Bridge — anything that speaks IMAP works out of the box.

Two-factor sign-in

Email-based one-time codes protect every login. No SMS pivots, no SIM-swap risk.

Encrypted at rest

App passwords are stored with Fernet (AES-128 + HMAC-SHA256) and only decrypted in memory when fetching mail.

One-tap connectivity tests

Power BI-style refresh per account or all at once, run in parallel so you know in seconds what's healthy.

Light & dark, on every device

Pixel-tuned for desktop and mobile. Respects your system preference, swap themes from any screen.

How it works

Three steps to a quieter inbox.

1

Sign in

Use your username and password. We email a 6-digit code to confirm the device.

2

Add your accounts

Generate an app-specific password from each provider, paste it once, encrypted and forgotten.

3

Read everything in one place

Recent messages from every mailbox, side by side. Search, filter by window, open and read.

Designed for the small screen

Looks great on every device.

Card-based layouts, full-screen drawer menu, hand-tuned typography. Built mobile-first, polished for desktop.

Security

Your inboxes are yours.

Privacy isn't a feature here, it's the default. Mail content is fetched on demand, nothing is cached server-side, and your IMAP credentials never leave your encrypted record.

Fernet-encrypted credentials. App passwords stored with AES-128-CBC + HMAC-SHA256.
Two-factor on every sign-in. Email OTP, expires in 10 minutes, locks after 5 wrong attempts.
No tracking, no analytics. No third-party scripts, no data sold or shared.
HTTPS everywhere. Secure cookies, HSTS, CSRF protection by default.

Built to be quietly secure.

Every connection to your provider uses TLS. Every credential is encrypted before it touches the database. Every sign-in needs the code we just emailed you. No surprises, no compromises.
